About Me

My Story

As a final year electronics engineering and computer science student, I have cultivated and applied the fundamentals varied programming with a clear focus on artificial intelligence. Through participating in AI-focused hackathons, I discovered my passion for artificial intelligence and began developing meaningful projects in the field. Now my tech stack has grown increasingly larger, allowing me to integrate my AI models into websites using JavaScript and CSS, automating them with pipelines utilizing ArgoCD and GitHub Actions, and deploy finished software applications with Vercel or Azure.

As of now, I am working on deepening my skills in AI, applying advanced techniques in projects such as NLP, Computer Vision and Reinforcement Learning. My goal is to design comprehensive AI tools using my expertise in these fields. My background in competitive swimming has imprinted discipline and goal-oriented thinking that I apply to my projects by setting clear objectives and making sure I complete them. Also, traveling whenever possible gives me inspiration for my next projects.
